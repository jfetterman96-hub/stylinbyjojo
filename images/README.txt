DROP YOUR IMAGES IN THIS FOLDER

The site automatically picks up images by filename — no code editing needed.
Just rename your photos to match these exact names and drop them in this
"images" folder, replacing nothing (the folder can be empty; placeholders
show automatically until you add real photos):

  hero.jpg         -> Big full-screen photo at the very top of the site.
                      Best as a tall/wide editorial shot. Recommended size:
                      1920x1280px or larger, landscape orientation.

  about.jpg         -> Photo of Jojo for the "About" section.
                      Recommended: portrait orientation, 1000x1250px.

  gallery-1.jpg
  gallery-2.jpg
  gallery-3.jpg
  gallery-4.jpg
  gallery-5.jpg
  gallery-6.jpg     -> The 6 lookbook/gallery photos. Portrait orientation
                      works best, roughly 1000x1300px each.

Tips:
- Keep filenames lowercase, exactly as above, ending in .jpg
  (if your photo is a .png or .jpeg, just rename the file extension to .jpg,
  or tell Claude and the code can be updated to match).
- Larger, well-lit, editorial-style photos will look the most "luxe."
- You can always add more gallery images later by duplicating a
  <div class="img-frame fade-up"> block in index.html.
