The Stylist

From the fast pace of New York to the effortless style of South Florida.

After spending years in New York, I made the move to South Florida, bringing my love for fashion and styling with me. Inspired by New York's fashion scene and South Florida's effortless lifestyle, I created my brand StylinbyJojo to make getting dressed feel easier, more enjoyable, and completely personalized to each client.

StylinbyJojo specializes in personal shopping and wardrobe styling, creating elevated, one-on-one shopping experiences tailored to your lifestyle. Whether you're looking to refresh your everyday wardrobe, style a special event, pack for a vacation, or build a closet you'll love for years to come, my goal is to help you feel confident, put together, and authentically yourself.
